Lasagna

Description

This lasagna was made out of nowhere, why? because it's just a simple recipe that anyone can make. It is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and béchamel sauce. The dish is baked until golden and bubbly, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. Perfect for family gatherings or a cozy night in, this lasagna is sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 medium red onions, finely diced
  • 2 large cloves minced garlic
  • 8 ounces pancetta, diced

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the onions and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  4. Stir in the pancetta and cook until browned.
  5. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish and bake for 45 minutes.
